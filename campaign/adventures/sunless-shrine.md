# ⛪ The Sunless Shrine — Authentic 5e Starter

*Breadth piece: proves the engine in the **by-the-book** system.*
**System:** 📚 Authentic D&D 5e (2024 / SRD 5.2) · **Levels:** 1 → 3 · **~30 min** ·
**Tone:** classic heroic dungeon (Mature optional) · **Freedom:** Guided (5 beats).

> **GM:** Run **strictly to SRD 5.2** (§8): real ability checks & DCs, **death saves**, short/long
> rests with Hit Dice, **spell slots** (not MP), **Weapon Mastery**, advantage/disadvantage (don't
> stack). This adventure exists to show the engine running *faithful* 5e. SRD stat blocks below —
> verify exact lines against SRD 5.2 if you want letter-perfect numbers (see §17 attribution).

## Premise
The sacred spring of **Hollow Ford** runs black, and those who drank are sickening. Beneath the
village's half-sunk old shrine, a desecrator — the ghoul-touched priest **Vorn Hule** — has fouled
the altar to feed a hunger in the dark. Descend, end the desecration, and **cleanse the spring**
before the village dies of thirst or worse.

## The Spine (Guided, 5 beats)
- **b1 Inciting** — the black spring; Elder Wenna begs the party to descend.
- **b2 Midpoint A** — the **shrine dungeon** (lookouts, the cistern, the nave).
- **b3 Midpoint B** — the **desecration revealed** (Vorn's cell + the defiled rites; a victim to save).
- **b4 Climax** — **Vorn Hule** at the altar (the boss).
- **b5 Resolution** — cleanse the altar/spring; the village's fate.

Initialise the Living-Map with `n0 = The Black Spring`.

## Cast
- **Elder Wenna** — the quester; calm, frightened, fair.
- **Pip-less** there's a **webbed survivor** (Toval, a scout) in the cistern — saving him = a future ally + intel.
- **Vorn Hule** — the ghoul-priest; once a healer, now a hollow thing that calls the spring "a mouth."

## Act 1 — The Black Spring *(b1)*
**DM:** The spring-water runs like ink and smells of the grave. Elder Wenna: *"Three are abed with
the shakes; the well's the only water for a day's walk. The old shrine below — something woke in it."*
- **Investigation/Religion DC 12** at the spring-mouth: the foulness is *channeled*, not natural — a
  rite, below. **Medicine DC 13**: the sickness is a wasting curse, reversible if the source is ended.
- Descent: a slick stair into the dark. (Light matters — track it, by the book.)

## Act 2 — The Shrine *(b2)*
1. **The Shrine Mouth.** **2 Goblins** keep watch (Nimble Escape). A **DC 14 Stealth** approach grants
   surprise; otherwise initiative. Weapon Mastery shines (Topple a goblin prone; Vex for advantage).
2. **The Cistern (skill + rescue).** Black water; **2 Giant Spiders** in the rafters; a **webbed
   survivor (Toval)** — **Str (Athletics) DC 13** to tear the webs, or burn them (careful of the
   victim). Saving Toval = intel on Vorn + the altar, and a grateful ally.
3. **The Desecrated Nave.** As the party crosses, **3 Skeletons** rise from the pews. **[Cleric] Turn
   Undead** is the intended answer; otherwise a real fight (skeletons are brittle — vulnerable to
   bludgeoning). A **short rest** is possible after, in a side-chapel (spend **Hit Dice**).

## Act 3 — Vorn's Cell & the Reveal *(b3)*
A monk's cell turned charnel-study. **Investigation DC 13** uncovers Vorn's journal: he was the
village healer; the spring "spoke" to him in a plague-year and he has been *feeding* it. A **trapped
reliquary** (**Perception DC 14** to spot, **Dex (Thieves' Tools) DC 14** to disarm (no tools/proficiency? raw **Dex DC 14**, or wedge it shut with Str DC 13 — a failed disarm springs the needle) — a poison-needle,
**DC 13 Con save** or 2d6 poison) holds a **Potion of Healing** + a silver holy symbol (the cleanse
focus). **(b3 resolved — the desecration is understood; the altar must be reconsecrated.)**

## Act 4 — The Altar: Vorn Hule *(b4 climax)*
**DM:** A drowned chapel; a black altar over the spring's source; **Vorn Hule**, grey-fleshed and
calm, mid-rite, two **Cultists** swaying beside him. *"You've come to take its mouth from me. It only
wants to be fed. Won't you feed it?"*

- **The fight:** Vorn (ghoul-priest, below) + 2 Cultists. **Ghoul paralysis is the danger** —
  **Con save DC 10** or paralyzed (by the book; a paralyzed PC is auto-crit in melee — *real stakes*).
  Expect a PC to go down → **death saves** in play. Position, Turn Undead, and focus-fire matter.
- **The win condition is two-fold (by the book):** drop Vorn **and reconsecrate the altar** — a
  **Religion check, DC 15** (advantage with the silver holy symbol, or a Cleric's Channel Divinity),
  taking an action while someone holds the line. Killing Vorn without cleansing leaves the spring
  fouled (a grimmer ending).

## Stat blocks (SRD 5.2 — verify exact lines)
- **Goblin** — AC 15, HP 7 (2d6), Speed 30. Scimitar **+4, 1d6+2**; shortbow **+4, 1d6+2**.
  *Nimble Escape:* Disengage/Hide as a bonus action.
- **Giant Spider** — AC 14, HP 26 (4d10+4), Speed 30/climb 30. Bite **+5, 1d8+3 + poison (Con DC 11,
  2d8, half on save)**; web (Dex DC 11 or restrained).
- **Skeleton** — AC 13, HP 13 (2d8+4). Shortsword **+4, 1d6+2**. Vulnerable to bludgeoning.
- **Cultist** — AC 12, HP 9 (2d8). Scimitar **+3, 1d6+1**.
- **Vorn Hule, Ghoul-Priest** *(SRD ghoul, augmented)* — AC 13, HP 36, Speed 30. **Multiattack:** bite
  **+6, 2d6+2**, and claws **+6, 2d4+2 + Con save DC 13 or paralyzed (1 min, save ends; a paralyzed
  PC within 5 ft is **auto-crit** from **round 2+**; a held/paralyzed target gets its end-of-turn save *before* Vorn's next melee, so a Cleric-less party isn't first-round-killed)**. **Round 1 he opens with Hold Person** on the front-liner to
  strip the tank — so the paralysis/death-save stakes actually bite. *Spellcasting
  (Wis, DC 13):* Cure Wounds (on himself), Inflict Wounds **+4, 3d10**, Hold Person. Undead — turnable.

## Endings *(b5)* & rewards
- **Cleansed** (Vorn down + altar reconsecrated): the spring runs clear by dawn; the sick recover;
  Hollow Ford holds a feast. **Level 3.**
- **Half-measure** (Vorn down, not cleansed): the village survives but the water stays brackish; a
  thread for later.
- **Rewards:** a **Potion of Healing**, the **silver holy symbol** (cleanse focus / +1 to a future
  reconsecration), ~50 gp, and Elder Wenna's standing aid.

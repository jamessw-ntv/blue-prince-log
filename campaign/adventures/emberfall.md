# 🔥 Emberfall — Flagship Rules-Light Heroic Campaign

*The showcase that proves the engine: put good detail in, and the game runs itself well.*
**System:** 🎲 Rules-Light · **Levels:** 2 → 4 · **Length:** ~30 min (one sitting) ·
**Tone:** Heroic fantasy with real weight · **Content:** **Mature (R)** — siege brutality,
sacrifice, a villain's tragedy; kept vivid not gratuitous; `dial it back` / `fade to black`
honoured instantly. · **Freedom dial:** **Guided** (5 locked beats, open between).

> **GM:** This is the *content layer*. Run it with the Rules-Light rules (§7) and the GM engine
> (§6); keep a `story` Living-Map (`STORY-STRUCTURE.md`); roll openly; fail forward. The design
> goal: whichever of the 6 classes the hero is, there is a beat that is **theirs**.

---

## Premise
The frontier town of **Emberfall** has held the Coldgate pass for a century. Tonight the warlord
**Sarn Vael, the Unbeaten**, arrives with his host — and he has *never lost a battle*, because
each victory is fed to the **Ember Heart**, a buried relic that trades him unnatural fortune for
the blood of the slain (his own dead included — he raises them). The party has **one night** to
break the siege from within: rally a breaking town, learn why Sarn cannot lose, and unmake it
before dawn.

## The Spine (locked beats — Guided)
- **b1 · Inciting** — *The horns.* The militia is buckling at the breach; Captain Mira begs the
  party to lead. (Trigger: the first assault hits.)
- **b2 · Midpoint A — The Approach** *(the big branch)* — choose how to reach Sarn's relic:
  **Walls / Shadows / Parley** (below). All converge on the Ember Heart.
- **b3 · Midpoint B — The Reveal** — discover the **pact**: Sarn is invincible while the Ember
  Heart beats, fed by the fallen. It must be *unmade*, not out-fought.
- **b4 · Climax — The Ember Heart** — break the pact (several methods) and face Sarn; the Heart
  offers **the party** the same bargain.
- **b5 · Resolution — Dawn** — what Emberfall costs, and who the party became.

Initialise the Living-Map with `n0 = The Breach` as the opening node; append as they go.

## Cast (NPC anchors)
- **Captain Mira Holt** — militia leader; iron will, wounded, holding the breach with her body.
  The town's heart. (If she falls, the town's morale clock speeds up.)
- **Sarn Vael, the Unbeaten** — the warlord. Proud, courteous, *tired of immortality*. A mature
  villain: not cruel for sport, but he will burn a town to keep his streak. A sliver of him wants
  to lose.
- **Hark** — Sarn's champion; honours a duel, despises a coward. (The Fighter's moment.)
- **Vey** — Sarn's conscript-captain; secretly sickened by the raising of the dead. Will defect
  if shown the truth or moved by a true voice. (The Bard/Wizard's moment.)
- **The Ember Heart** — a fist-sized coal that *beats*. It is the antagonist behind the antagonist.

## Act 1 — The Breach *(b1, ~5 min)*
**DM:** Horns. The east wall is a wound of torchlight and screaming; conscripts pour at a gap in
the stone. **Captain Mira**, blood down one arm, grabs the hero: *"You — you've the look of someone
who finishes things. Hold this breach or Emberfall's ash by dawn. Tell me what you need."*

- **Rally the militia** — a breaking line. **[★ Bard]** Performance/CHA **DC 13** (or Inspiration)
  steadies them: militia gain **+1d4** to hold this scene. **[★ Cleric]** a blessing does the same;
  **[★ Fighter]** leading from the front (Athletics **DC 13**) does it by example.
- **The first wave** — **4 Conscripts** hit the gap (stats below). A sharp fight to set stakes.
  **[★ Ranger]** Hunter's Volley thins them; **[★ Rogue]** picks off the sergeant; **[★ Wizard]**
  Magic Missile / a clever Shield.
- **The horn that doesn't stop.** When the wave breaks, a single warhorn sounds three times and the
  *town's own dead from the wall begin to stand.* (First hint of the pact — **b3** foreshadowed.)

**Mira's ask sets the fork:** *"Sarn's in his command tent past the gate. Whatever lets him do…
that —"* she nods at the rising dead *"— it's with him. Get to it. There's three ways out of this
wall, and I can buy you one."*

## Act 2 — The Approach *(b2, the big branch, ~12 min)*
Three routes to Sarn's command circle and the Ember Heart. **Pick one; they converge.** Each route
is built around two class spotlights but any class can run it (alternative checks given).

### Route A — The Walls (assault) **[★ Fighter, ★ Cleric]**
Punch out through the sally and cut a path. Brutal, loud, fast.
- **Hold-and-advance:** waves of **Conscripts** (2–3 at a time). Fail-forward: losing ground costs
  HP and time, never the route.
- **The Risen rise behind you.** **[★ Cleric]** Channel Divinity turns them (WIS DC 13 each) — the
  *intended* answer; without a cleric, they must be re-dropped (they ash at 0, skip the dying clock)
  or outrun (Athletics **DC 13**).
- **Hark bars the gate.** Sarn's champion: *"One of you. Blade to blade. Win and the gate's yours."*
  **[★ Fighter]** a real duel (Hark stats below) — Action Surge/Second Wind shine. Any class may
  duel, ally, or refuse (refusing = Hark and two Risen, harder). Beating Hark earns the gate **and**
  his respect (he may warn you: *"He cannot be killed. I've watched it.")* → seeds **b3**.

### Route B — The Shadows (infiltration) **[★ Rogue, ★ Ranger]**
The cliff goat-path Mira's scouts use; in through the camp's blind side.
- **The cliff path:** **[★ Ranger]** Survival **DC 13** finds it cleanly; others climb (Athletics
  **DC 15**, fail = swept-style setback + noise).
- **The picket:** slip the guards. **[★ Rogue]** Stealth **DC 14** (advantage if scouted); a caught
  approach turns it into Route-A fighting (fail-forward, not a dead end). **[★ Ranger]** a called
  shot drops the alarm-bell guard.
- **The ember-ward:** the relic's tent is sealed by a sigil that *burns the unworthy*. **[★ Rogue]**
  Sleight of Hand/Thieves' DC 15 to trace and bypass it; **[★ Wizard]** Arcana **DC 14** reads it
  instead — *and reveals the pact* (→ **b3** here). A failed bypass = a burn (2d6 fire, fail-forward
  inside).

### Route C — The Parley (subterfuge) **[★ Bard, ★ Wizard]**
Walk in under a flag and turn the knife from within.
- **Audience with Sarn:** he grants courtesy to the bold. **[★ Bard]** read the room, stall, and
  work **Vey**, his conscript-captain (Insight **DC 13** spots Vey's disgust at the raising).
- **Turn Vey:** **[★ Bard]** Persuasion **DC 15** (or **[★ Wizard]** expose the pact-sigil on Vey's
  own conscription-brand, Arcana **DC 14**) flips him — Vey opens a path to the Heart and pulls his
  men back at the climax. This is the route that can *save the most lives*.
- **Sarn's tell:** in conversation his invincibility, the raising, and his weariness all surface
  (→ **b3** delivered as dialogue). He lets the party near the Heart because *he wants to be tested.*

> **Converge:** all three routes arrive at **The Ember Circle** with the **b3 Reveal** in hand —
> *Sarn cannot be beaten while the Ember Heart beats; it is fed by the fallen; it must be unmade.*

## Act 3 — The Ember Heart *(b4 climax + b5 resolution, ~10 min)*
**DM:** A ring of black banners. At its centre, a coal the size of a fist floats and **beats**, slow
as a sleeping thing, ringed by an **ember-ward**. Sarn stands beside it, sword sheathed. *"You came
far. Few do. Show me you're worth the loss."*

**Two problems at once:** Sarn (unkillable while the Heart beats) and the Heart (untouchable behind
the ward). The party must **drop the ward → unmake the Heart**, surviving Sarn meanwhile.

### Dropping the ward — many keys (class spotlights)
- **[★ Wizard]** *Unweave it.* Arcana/Dispel **DC 15** (2 successes, or one with Misty Step inside).
- **[★ Cleric]** *Consecrate it.* Channel Divinity, **3 rounds** of holding ground — a clock that
  makes the fight matter.
- **[★ Bard]** *Turn his own men.* If Vey defected (Route C), the conscripts refuse to feed it; the
  ward **starves** and gutters in 2 rounds. Or Vicious Mockery + a public truth shames Sarn into
  *dropping his guard* (CHA contest).
- **[★ Rogue]** *Steal the keystone.* A focus-gem socketed in the ward — Sleight of Hand **DC 16**
  under fire pulls it.
- **[★ Ranger]** *Sever the feed.* The Heart draws from corpse-cairns at the ring's edge; Hunter's
  Volley / fire on the cairns (DC 13 ×3) cuts its fuel.
- **[★ Fighter]** *Brute the brazier.* The ward anchors to an iron brazier — Athletics **DC 15** to
  topple it into the snow drops the ward by force.

### Unmaking the Heart (b4) — once the ward is down
The Heart, AC 15, HP 20, but it **speaks**, and it offers *the party* the bargain: *"Be unbeaten.
I ask so little — only the ones you'd have lost anyway."* The moral fork (mature):
- **Destroy it** *(heroic)* — shatter the coal. Sarn, mortal at last, may **yield** (his weariness)
  or make a true last stand. Costly: the longer the fight ran, the more of the town/Mira is at risk.
- **Take the bargain** *(dark)* — claim the Heart. The siege breaks instantly — because *you* are
  Unbeaten now. Emberfall is saved, and the party is its new shadow. A genuine, playable dark ending.
- **Turn the pact** *(clever)* — feed the Heart **Sarn's own victories/name** (set up via Bard/Vey
  or Wizard's reading): it consumes its maker. He dies *unmade*, the Heart goes cold, no party cost
  — the hard-to-reach best ending.

### Sarn Vael in the climax
While the Heart beats he has **advantage on everything and returns at half-HP** the round after he
drops. The instant the Heart is unmade, strip both — he's just a tired, deadly man (and may yield).

## Stat blocks (Rules-Light)
- **Conscript** — HP 7, AC 13, **Init +1**. Spear **+3, 1d6+1**. Breaks at half-numbers if their
  sergeant falls.
- **The Risen** (raised dead) — HP 10, AC 11, **Init +0**. Claw **+3, 1d6+1**. **Mindless** —
  crumble to ash at 0 (skip the dying clock); **turned** by Cleric Channel Divinity.
- **Hark, Champion** — HP 30, AC 16, **Init +1**. Greataxe **+6, 1d12+4**. *Unbeaten Roar*
  (recharge 5–6): 20 ft, **WIS DC 13** or **Frightened** (save ends). Honourable — yields at 0 if
  the duel was fair.
- **Sarn Vael** — HP 45, AC 17, **Init +2**. Sword, **multiattack 2 × 1d8+4 (+7)**. *Ember Boon*
  (while the Heart beats): advantage on all d20s; at 0 HP he revives to 23 HP at the start of his
  next turn. *Unmade* (Heart destroyed): loses the Boon entirely; may surrender.
- **The Ember Heart** — object, AC 15, HP 20, immune while the **ember-ward** holds (drop it first,
  see Act 3).

## Endings (b5) & rewards
- **Dawn Unbroken** (destroy, low cost): the town holds; Mira lives; Sarn buried as a man. Title
  **"Wardens of Coldgate."**
- **Dawn at a Price** (destroy, ran long / Mira fell): saved, but a funeral pyre at sunrise.
- **The New Unbeaten** (bargain): Emberfall kneels to its saviours; the party carries the Heart —
  and its hunger. *(Sequel hook; mature.)*
- **The Unmade Warlord** (turn the pact): Sarn crumbles, the Heart goes cold, no one else pays.
- **Rewards:** level to **4**; loot — **Sarn's Sundered Blade** (Rare; +1, once/fight reroll a miss)
  or an **Ember-Cinder** (Rare relic shard: a boon with a cost, if taken); the town's gratitude.

Assumptions baked in

Everything above ground (fly mod), and MAX output: every machine at 250% (3 Power Shards) + a Somersloop (2×) = 5× each, so counts are ~⅕. Toggle ⚡ MAX off in Blueprints to see 100% counts. Far districts (Aluminium / Nuclear / Quantum) are railed in.

Glossary — the words this plan uses

TermWhat it means
DistrictA self-contained mini-base grouped around one material family (Ironworks, Copperworks…). Each base material is made once, sized to total demand, and shared. 7 production districts + ★ Project Assembly.
Bank / area (§N)One production cell inside a district: N copies of the same machine making one item (e.g. §3 · Screw farm = several Constructors making screws).
Processing stageHow far down the chain a bank sits. The placement view lays stages out left → right, so raw-ish parts are on the left and finished parts on the right.
Manifold rowOne input belt feeding a row of machines through splitters, their outputs merging back onto one belt. It's the repeating unit you blueprint once and stack.
Belt web / exact placementThe machine-level drill-down: every machine, splitter (▫), merger (◇), belt segment and storage buffer (🗄) for one bank. Click any area block to open it.
Splitter / mergerThe logistics piece that taps a belt off to a machine (splitter) or combines machine outputs back onto one belt (merger).
Storage buffer (🗄)A container between stages that evens out flow so a line never starves or backs up.
Overclock + SomersloopRunning a machine at 250% (3 Power Shards) and slotting a Somersloop (2× output) = 5× per machine, so you build ~⅕ the machines. That's the ⚡ MAX toggle; turn it off for 100% counts.
Trunk / laneThe main belt of a flow. When its rate tops a belt's throughput, you split it into parallel lanes (e.g. "2× Mk.6") and feed the manifold from both sides.
PhaseA Space Elevator delivery milestone (1–5). Each completed phase unlocks the next tech tier and the next districts.

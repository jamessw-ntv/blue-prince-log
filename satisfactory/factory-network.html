Assumptions baked in

Everything above ground (fly mod), and MAX output: every machine at 250% (3 Power Shards) + a Somersloop (2×) = 5× each, so counts are ~⅕. Toggle ⚡ MAX off in Blueprints to see 100% counts. Far districts (Aluminium / Nuclear / Quantum) are railed in.

Belts & pipes — when you get them, when to upgrade

Each build step prints the exact belt Mk for that line and flags upgrade when a trunk outgrows its belt. Lay the best belt you have unlocked, then come back and upgrade the fat trunks as new Mks arrive. Rough unlock timing:

CarrierThroughputYou'll have it by…

If a trunk needs more than your best belt can carry, split it across two belts until the next Mk unlocks. Fluids (oil, water, alumina, nitrogen, heavy oil residue) always go on pipes, never belts.
